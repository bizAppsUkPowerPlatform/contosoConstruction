Repairs

  • Common repairs will often need to be organised in tenements and other buildings with multiple owners.
  • This involves all owners in the building agreeing to the work and sharing its cost.
  • Some buildings have a scheme or a factor to help with this.

Design

When it comes to building design, Contoso's architects have an essential job.

  • Their principal responsibility is to create an aesthetically pleasing and effective building design that meets the client’s expectations.
  • An artistic vision will drive an architect’s design decisions, but architects are not just designers concerned with style.
  • They are problem solvers working to develop a design that meets the client’s needs to be both practical and aesthetically appealing.
